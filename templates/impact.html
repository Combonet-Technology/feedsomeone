Our Impact

Community action since 2019

Small beginnings, practical support

Our early outreach activities were funded by the founder, friends and volunteers. We are documenting this history transparently while building stronger systems for measurement and reporting.

Feed Someone 1.0

27 December 2019 · Akure, Ondo State

Friends and volunteers prepared and distributed meals and drinks to more than 150 people in an underserved street-connected community.

View gallery

Feed Someone 2.0

December 2020 · Akure, Ondo State

Two children's homes and an underserved street-connected community received food staples, infant supplies, toiletries, clothing, footwear and other relief materials collected through a community campaign.

View gallery

Children's outreach support

2021 · Abeokuta, Ogun State

The founder supported an independently led outreach reported to have reached approximately 200 children. Partner and contribution details are being documented.

Birthday orphanage outreach

9 August 2022 · Lugbe, Abuja

A founder-funded visit delivered noodles, beverages and other essential supplies to a children's home with support from a small group of friends.
