Support our noble causes

Hunger and health disparities are pressing challenges in our community, especially for orphans. At the Oluwafemi Ebenezer Foundation, we're committed to comprehensive support, addressing hunger, healthcare, and mental well-being.

Orphanage Visits:

We regularly visit orphanages, spreading love and support. With your help, we provide essential items like food, clothing, educational materials, and toys to bring joy to the children's lives.

Medical Outreaches:

We conduct medical outreaches, offering free check-ups, consultations, and medications. Your support extends our reach, ensuring essential healthcare access for more people.

Mental Health Aid:

We provide counseling and support services to address trauma, anxiety, and depression, aiming to restore hope and resilience in our community.

Health Program Sensitization:

We empower our community with health education, promoting hygiene and disease prevention. Informed individuals make healthier choices.

Together, we build a healthier, happier, and more prosperous community. Join us in the fight for improved health and against hunger, contributing to a brighter future for all.
