About our foundation

From Feed Someone to a broader platform for opportunity

Founded from the Feed Someone outreach conceived in 2019, the Oluwafemi Ebenezer Foundation is a registered Nigerian nonprofit working to meet urgent needs while expanding long-term opportunity.

Feed Someone remains our flagship relief programme. OEF is the legal foundation behind the wider mission: orphanage relief, community support, health and dignity, safe lives, learning access and future digital opportunity.

Mission: To provide practical relief, learning support, health awareness, dignity-focused care and empowerment opportunities for underserved children, families and communities.

Vision: A society where poverty, hunger, abuse, preventable disease and lack of opportunity no longer define a person's future.
