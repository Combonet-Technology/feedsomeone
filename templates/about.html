About US

What we are doing

We Are On A Mission To Provide The Following To The Helpless

Feeding

We are here to help the less-privileged have access to good food and clean water.

Health

We provide health-care assistance to the less-privilege, both young and old

Shelter

We provide a place of shelter to the displaced until they are able to get their own places

Empowerment

We provide education, human development and capital to the less privilege.

About our foundation

We are on a mission to eradicate hunger and poverty

Mission Statement: To ensure that the marginalized, socially and economically unstable are being kept in a state of hapiness and vitality by a steady source of meal and shelter

Vision Statement: Transforming the percentage of disadvantaged and mentally unstable people into valuable members of the society with the aid of rehabilitating actions such as free feeding, shelter, counselling and empowerment.
