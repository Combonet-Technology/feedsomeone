About our foundation

From Feed Someone to a broader platform for opportunity

Founded from the Feed Someone outreach conceived in 2019, the Oluwafemi Ebenezer Foundation is a Nigerian nonprofit working to meet urgent needs while expanding long-term opportunity.

Mission: To improve the lives of underserved children, families and communities in Nigeria through practical relief, preventive health, protection, education, empowerment and access to technology.

Vision: A world where every child and underserved community has the health, protection, resources and opportunities required to live with dignity and build a hopeful future.
