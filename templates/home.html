Oluwafemi Ebenezer Foundation is a registered Nigerian nonprofit founded from the Feed Someone movement. We support underserved children, orphanages, families and communities through practical relief, health awareness, learning support, safety, dignity and opportunity-focused programmes.

What we are doing

Programmes that meet urgent needs and expand future opportunity

Feed Someone

Our flagship relief programme provides food and essential supplies to underserved communities and children's homes.

Healthy Communities

We promote disease prevention, health education and access to qualified healthcare partners.

Safe Lives

We advance child protection, abuse prevention, mental-health awareness and trusted referral pathways.

Empower Futures

We create pathways to education, skills, mentorship, technology and sustainable livelihoods.

About our foundation

Building dignity, resilience and hope

Feed Someone began in 2019 as a practical response to hunger and hardship. OEF now carries that conviction forward as a legal foundation focused on immediate relief and longer-term opportunity.

Mission: To provide practical relief, learning support, health awareness, dignity-focused care and empowerment opportunities for underserved children, families and communities.

Vision: A society where poverty, hunger, abuse, preventable disease and lack of opportunity no longer define a person's future.
