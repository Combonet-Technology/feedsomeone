Transparency and Governance

Accountability as we grow

Building public trust through clear information

Oluwafemi Ebenezer Foundation is a Nigerian nonprofit registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission. Our programmes began through founder funding and volunteer support, and we have not yet received an institutional grant.

Legal registration

  • Legal name: Oluwafemi Ebenezer Foundation
  • CAC registration number: 179189
  • Date of incorporation: 27 April 2022
  • Country of registration: Nigeria

What we publish

  • Clear descriptions of our programmes and activities.
  • Conservative beneficiary figures, with estimates identified as estimates.
  • Funding sources for activities, including founder-funded work.
  • Corrections when previously published information is incomplete or inaccurate.

Governance information in preparation

Trustee profiles, safeguarding policy, conflict-of-interest policy, financial controls summary and reporting documents are being prepared for publication after verification.

Funding history

OEF's completed activities have been primarily founder-funded and volunteer-supported. Older annual-report drafts exist for internal planning and past application preparation, but they should not be treated as audited public accounts until reconciled with receipts, bank records and formal accounting review.

Contact and verification

Organizations conducting due diligence may contact info@oluwafemiebenezerfoundation.org.
